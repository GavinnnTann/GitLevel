The calling

Every language is a class.

Your most-used language sets your profession; your second becomes a subclass. A crest, a colour, a lineage of titles — all your own.

The ascent

Craft is the only currency.

XP comes from what you build — commits, PRs, reviews, issues, repos — amplified by your years on GitHub. Popularity is a separate axis. No shortcuts to prestige.
